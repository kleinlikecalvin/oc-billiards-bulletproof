A typical league night

FIRST! Show up at the host location. Then! Walk in and order a drink. Practice a little to warm up. Have a drink. Wait for your turn to play. You guessed it! Have another drink but this time do it while cheering for your teammates. Get a little nervous when it’s your turn to play so naturally if you haven't blacked out yet one would have another drink to "calm your nerves." Play your match and hang out with your teammates while the other matches finish, all while drinking. Then pay your league dues and take a cab home. The morning is tomorrows problem! Lol or just hang out and learn and experience and fall in love with everything that playing in a league with new friends can offer!
